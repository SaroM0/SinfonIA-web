HEY THERE!

We are the SinfonIA team

We are the robotics and AI research group at Universidad de Los Andes, a team passionate about merging technology and human interaction. We currently work in the following subgroups: Perception, Navigation, Manipulation and Speech, each of which refines a piece of the social interaction pie. Likewise, members in Interface and Toolkit ensure that our robots are top-notch, while task members orchestrate the grand symphony of robotic tasks. We are a multidisciplinary dream team, merging engineering prowess with the creative minds of several university departments. Join us on this journey where technology and humanity come together to shape the future of robotics.

Our vision

We aspire to forge a future where technology and research merge into a perfect symphony. Our vision encompasses leadership in orchestrating innovation in emerging fields, fostering collaboration that resonates with diversity and creativity. We seek to impact society significantly, transcending beyond research to improve lives and understand our world. We strive to stand out as leaders in scientific harmonization, where each member contributes to a collective composition of excellence and originality. We envision the construction of a constant technological crescendo, taking our research to new heights and ensuring a lasting impact on society, convinced that true innovation arises from the perfect union of creative minds and transformative technology.
