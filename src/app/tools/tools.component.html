Meet our team!

We are made up of individuals from diverse academic disciplines who share a common interest: to explore beyond conventional boundaries and immerse ourselves in the fascinating world of robotics, artificial intelligence, machine learning, programming, and many other exciting areas that our group has to offer. We are committed to learning and applying this knowledge in practice, with the goal of expanding our skills and making a significant contribution to the advancement of technology.

Tools

We've developed seven tools, meticulously organized into subgroups, form a strategic framework for seamless communication with the robot. The toolkit initiates direct communication, while other tools oversee the robot's senses, navigation, arm manipulation, environment interaction, speech processing, perception through cameras, and interface interaction via a tablet. This integration ensures not only comprehensive control over the robot's diverse functionalities but also enhances its adaptability and intelligence in executing tasks across various scenarios.

Task Team

Task Team is a dedicated group specializing in the development of state-machines. Our core objective involves aggregating services from diverse tools, seamlessly integrating them to empower robots in the execution and strategic planning of specific tasks. Presently, our endeavors extend to projects aimed at achieving comprehensive automation through cutting-edge AI technologies, enabling the robots to autonomously program and optimize their functionalities.
